About me

Back Story

Growing up, I wasn't one of those kids who knew exactly what he wanted to be (i.e. Astronaut, Police Office, Cowboy etc.), instead I focused on what I love doing which was playing football. I played football most of my life and when I realized I could not play forever, I was stuck with what to do with my career. It wasn't until I built my first computer from scratch that I knew working with computers was where I belong. It was the ability to teach myself how to use & create technology that formed my path in life. Hence, I earned a Masters Degree from DeVry in Management Information Systems and began working in the IT field for a Network Consultant for two years.

Continued...

From there I created my own business repairing smartphone and tablet screens in coordination with a company based in CA, iCracked. I have recently completed my journey through Dev Bootcamp, a 19-week intensive training program for aspiring web-developers, specializing in Ruby, Rails, HTML5, CSS, Test Driven Development, Object-Oriented Javascript, (AJAX, JQuery), Git and Source Control, Heroku and Agile Development. This program has turned me into a Full-Stack Web Developer. I now plan to fulfill my desire to create beautiful and meaningful things.
